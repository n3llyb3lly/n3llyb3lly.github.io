Music + Sound

Original compositions and soundtracks.

The Cold

A cathartic blend of Ecuadorian roots and American indie folk.
The Cold is the first song I’m genuinely proud of, both in terms of production and lyrics. I tried to combine my Ecuadorian roots with my love for American indie folk and rock to create a darker, more cathartic sound. This track captures the direction I want to take my future music.

Software Used
  • Cakewalk by BandLab
  • Various VSTs: Synth 1, TyrellN6, Valhalla, etc.
  • Mostly stock Cakewalk effects (reverb, delay, etc.)
Timeline of Production

Started in July 2023, completed in January 2024. I plan to fully rerecord this track in the future.

Behind-the-Scenes Notes

This track was a massive learning curve. The mix is still a bit rough, but I gained a lot of skills in mixing and mastering along the way. I used some unconventional sound sources—like a juggling ball as a shaker—and experimented with layered synths to create that thick, gloomy atmosphere. The lyrics are very personal and reflect the kind of emotional numbness that hits during the darkest parts of a Minnesota winter.

Superstar (V2.)

A sleazy top 40s disco hit from the future.
Superstar (V2.) was commissioned by Adiana Contreras for her film Asphodel. She wanted something that sounded like it came from a washed-up star named Chip Creep (that’s me). I had a blast creating this track—it’s catchy, fun, over-the-top, and was a perfect excuse to seriously dive into Ableton.

Software Used
  • Ableton Live 12 Suite
  • Various VSTs: Synth 1, TyrellN6, Valhalla, etc.
  • Mostly stock Ableton effects (reverb, delay, etc.)
Timeline of Production

Started in August 2024 and completed in October 2024 for the film. I’m still making minor tweaks for its release.

Behind-the-Scenes Notes

This track was all about experimenting with a retro-futuristic sound. I leaned hard into 80s-inspired analog synth patches using Synth 1 by Daichi, TyrellN6 by u-he, and native Ableton drum racks—especially the 707 kit—to achieve that timeless, punchy vibe.

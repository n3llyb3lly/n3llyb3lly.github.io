Music + Sound

Original compositions and soundtracks.

The Cold

The Cold was completed using open source software (Cakewalk) and plugins. Though I have since moved to Ableton, this is my first song that I am proud of production-wise and lyrically. I tried to combine my Ecuadorian roots with my love for American indie folk and rock to make a darker and cathartic sound. The Cold is more like what I want to create in the future.

Software Used
  • Cakewalk by BandLab
  • Various VSTs (Synth 1, TyrellN6, Valhalla etc.)
  • Mostly stock Cakewalk effects (reverb, delay, etc.)
Timeline of Production

Started in July 2023, completed in January 2024. I plan on rerecording it completely in the future.

Behind-the-Scenes Notes

This song was a huge learning experience for me. Though it is still roughly mixed, I gained a lot mixing and mastering skills in the process. I used a lot of unconventional sound sources (like a juggling ball as a shaker) and experimented with layering different synths to create a thick, gloomy atmospheric sound. The lyrics are deeply personal, reflecting the feelings of despair one often experiences in the dark days of a Minnesota winter.

Superstar (V2.)

Superstar (V2.) was commissioned by Adiana Contreras for her amazing film Asphodel. She wanted a sleazy top 40s disco hit from the future sang by the washed up Chip Creep (me). I had a blast making this track, and it was a great opportunity to seriously experiment with Ableton. The song is meant to be catchy, fun, and over-the-top, just like Chip!

Software Used
  • Ableton Live 12 Suite
  • Various VSTs (Synth 1, TyrellN6, Valhalla etc.)
  • Mostly stock Ableton effects (reverb, delay, etc.)
Timeline of Production

Started in August 2024, completed in October 2024 for the film, though I am still making minor tweaks for its release.

Behind-the-Scenes Notes

This track is an experiment in creating a retro-futuristic sound. I used a lot of 80s-inspired analog synth patches on Synth 1 by Daichi, and TyrellN6 by u-he, along with native Ableton patches and drum racks (707 is the main one) to achieve that timeless vibe.

Higher Living

Two friends, a radio, and a quest for alien contact. Higher Living is a short film that explores the themes of unconditional friendship, curiosity, and the enjoyment of the little things. It was created as part of an off-campus study in Los Angeles. I cowrote the script, got most of the props, worked as a secondary editor, and handled all of the sound: recording, design, scoring and editing. The film was shot in only two days (eight hours total), and with a super tight budget. We also tried to be super discreet while filming because we had issues with permits. The film was shot in the Hollywood Hills, close to Griffith Park.

Software Used
  • Adobe Premiere Pro (for editing and sound mixing)
  • Ableton Live 12 (for scoring)
Timeline of Production

Started and completed in May 2025.

Behind-the-Scenes Notes

This film was a huge learning experience for me. I had never worked on a collaborative film before, and I learned a lot about sound design, recording, and editing. The radio was a little heater I found at a thrift store, and after we taped a crochet hook to it, it ended up being the perfect centerpiece for the film. We found our wonderful actors on Backstage and had a great time working with them.

Hinterland

A Visual Novel in an Endless Forest

Game Description

Hinterland is a psychological horror visual novel built in Ren'Py, combining folk horror, branching logic, and a unique personality-humor mechanic that shapes story, visuals, and UI.

Development Notes

  • Custom stat-tracking and dialogue systems
  • Hand-drawn sprites (inspired by Don't Starve)
  • Exploration of change, decay, and self-undoing
